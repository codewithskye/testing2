About Me

Hi, I’m Daniel Owolabi a UI/UX designer, web designer, data analyst enthusiast, and crypto/forex technical analyst based in Nigeria. With over three years of experience, I focus on creating user-friendly, intuitive designs that solve real problems and deliver smooth digital experiences.

My journey started with a passion for mixing creativity and tech. I’ve worked on all kinds of projects from designing mobile apps for startups to building responsive websites for growing brands. I’ve picked up skills in UI/UX design, web development, data analysis, graphics design, video editing, and even a bit of Python programming.

I always design with the user in mind every decision is backed by research and a deep understanding of what users need. Whether I’m sketching out ideas in Figma, coding with JavaScript, or digging into data for insights, I aim to create things that are both beautiful and useful.

Outside of work, I like checking out the latest design trends, researching new ideas, watching movies, and doing crypto airdrops. I enjoy working on cool projects with great people, so if you want to learn more about web design, UI/UX, or even crypto, feel free to reach out. Let’s build something awesome together!
